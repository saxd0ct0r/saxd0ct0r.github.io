Tempo Trainer

Interactive Practice Tool

by Timothy J. Owen, D.M.A.

Welcome to Tempo Trainer! This tool helps you systematically find and improve your tempo using a divide-and-conquer search. After each attempt it narrows in on your limit in fewer and fewer steps.

First session: Leave the tempo field empty. The trainer starts at 60 BPM and homes in on your current ability.

Later sessions: Enter the tempo you reached last time. The trainer first confirms you can still hit that mark (review), then pushes you faster (growth).

First time using the tool? Leave the field empty and hit Begin. On later sessions, enter the tempo you reached last time.
