Tempo Trainer

Interactive Practice Tool

by Timothy J. Owen, D.M.A.

Welcome to Tempo Trainer! This tool helps you systematically improve your tempo performance using an efficient binary search algorithm.

Untargeted Mode: Perfect for scales and exercises without a specific tempo goal. The trainer helps you discover your maximum tempo.

Targeted Mode: Ideal for pieces with specific tempo markings. The trainer helps you identify trouble spots and build up to your goal tempo.
